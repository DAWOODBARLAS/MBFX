How does copy trading work?

Copy trading, a form of “social trading,” enables investors to replicate the actions of expert traders, often through paid subscriptions. With copy trading, the process is fully automated, eliminating the need for users to manually search for signals from experts. By linking their accounts to the copy trading platform, all the work is handled by the platform in real-time, staying within the designated budget. Users simply need to monitor their copy trading portfolio!
